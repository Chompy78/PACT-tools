    ℹ️ PACT Character Generator — Tool Guide

    What this tool does

    This is the builder. Use it to design a character from scratch using the PACT points-buy system — choose species, ability scores, HD, skills, feats, class features, spells, and more. Every choice has an AP cost drawn from the master price list. The budget counter and legality check update live.

    Saving your build

    • 💾 Save downloads a .json snapshot you can reload later with 📂 Load.
    • 🔗 Share encodes the entire build into the page URL — copy and send it to share with anyone.

    Sending to the Live Sheet

    When your build is ready to play, click ⇆ Live Sheet. This downloads a -livesheet.json file that converts your snapshot into an event log — every purchase becomes a timestamped entry. Open the Live Sheet and use ⬆ Import to load it. After that, the Live Sheet takes over: you spend AP there during play, and prices are frozen at the values they had when you bought them.
