ℹ️ PACT Live Character Sheet — Tool Guide

What this tool does

This is the player tool. It tracks your character through actual play using an event-sourced log — every AP award and purchase is recorded in order, so you have a full history and can undo/redo any step. Prices are frozen at the moment of purchase: rules updates never change what you already paid.

Getting a character in

  • Build your character in the PACT Character Generator, then click ⇆ Live Sheet to download a -livesheet.json file.
  • Click ⬆ Import here to load that file. Your full purchase history is reconstructed from it.
  • Alternatively, start fresh — the sheet seeds a blank level-1 character automatically.

During play

  • Use DM tools → + Award AP to add points after each session.
  • Buy new features from the Buy / Progress panel — each purchase is appended to the log.
  • ↶ Undo / ↷ Redo step through your history. The time-travel slider lets you view any past state without changing it.

Saving & sharing

  • The sheet auto-saves to your browser's local storage after every action.
  • ⬇ Export downloads a portable .json backup you can move between devices or share with your DM.
  • 📋 Sheet shows a printable character sheet; 📄 A4 Sheet opens a print-ready page.

Version notes

If you import a file built under an older rules version, the sheet warns you and keeps those prices frozen — future purchases use the current price list.
